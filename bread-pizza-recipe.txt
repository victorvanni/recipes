Ingredients:
 - 6 slices of bread
 - Tomato salsa
 - Cheese
 - Oregano

Directions:
 1. Preheat oven to 200 degrees C.
 2. Put the slices of bread in a metal recipient.
 3. Put the salsa over each slice of bread.
 4. Put the cheese over each slice of bread.
 5. Sprinkle oregano over the bread.
 6. Take the form to the oven until the bottom of the bread is lightly browned.
